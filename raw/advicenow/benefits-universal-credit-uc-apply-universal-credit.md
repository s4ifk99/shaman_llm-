#  Apply for Universal Credit

Information about who is eligibile for Universal Credit and how to make a claim successfully. Also explains how to challenge a decision about your claim. If you want to challenge a decision about whether you are capable of work or not, please use our [work capability assessment mandatory reconsideration letter tool](/get-help/benefits/universal-credit-uc/work-capability-assessment-mandatory-reconsideration-letter "WCA mandatory reconsideration letter tool") to help you make your case.
