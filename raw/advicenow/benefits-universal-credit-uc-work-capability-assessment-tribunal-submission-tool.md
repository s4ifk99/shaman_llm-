#  Work Capability Assessment Tribunal Submission Tool

Welcome to our Work Capability Assessment (WCA) Tribunal Submission tool to help you write a submission for the tribunal that explains why the decision the DWP made about the claim for Employment and Support Allowance or the limited capability for work element of Universal Credit claim was wrong. This tool is designed to be used by individuals appealing their own work capability assessment decision, as well as advisers and volunteers helping claimants. We hope that having a submission will make it easier for you on the day of your hearing.

Please complete the form below. The words already in the boxes will appear in the submission you will send to the tribunal - you just need to finish the sentence.

We will use the information to create a submission for you to send to the First Tier Tribunal, ahead of the hearing. You can upload it using the Manage your appeal service or send it by post.

All the information you supply is in total confidence. We will keep the details secure and will never pass them on to anyone. You can read more in our [Privacy notice](https://www.advicenow.org.uk/about-us/about-our-site/terms-and-conditions?anchor=privacyNotice).
