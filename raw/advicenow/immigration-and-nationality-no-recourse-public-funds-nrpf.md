#  No recourse to public funds (NRPF)

Some migrants to the UK have 'no recourse to public funds', which means that they cannot access certain benefits or support provided by the government or local council. Includes what counts as public funds, who has no recourse to public funds, and what can be done about it - links reviewed and selected by Advicenow.
