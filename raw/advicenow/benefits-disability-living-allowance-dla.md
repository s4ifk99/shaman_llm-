#  Disability Living Allowance (DLA)

Here you will find all our resources to help you get the right amount of Disability Living Allowance (DLA) for your child if they have a health condition, impairment, or disability.

We explain how entitlement to Disability Living Allowance works and what to do if you get a decision ou think is unfair.  We have a tool to support you to ask for a DLA mandatory reconsideration effectively, and if that doesn't work, we have all the help you need to appeal and win.

We also have a guide for professionals writing evidence for DLA applications or appeals, and step-by-step help to write a statement for the tribunal.

According to research, 90% of people who used our tools and guides to challenge a decision succeeded in getting the decision changed at the mandatory reconsideration stage or tribunal.
