#  Find the user guide for your smart meter's in-home display

An in-home display (IHD) is a small electronic screen that connects wirelessly to your gas and electricity smart meters. You can use it to see how much energy you’re using and how much it costs. [Find out how to use your in-home display to save energy](/consumer/energy/energy-supply/your-energy-meter/using-the-energy-monitor-for-your-smart-meter/).

Your IHD's user guide can help you:

  * understand how it works

  * fix common problems

You can also [check our guide to fixing common problems with your in-home display](/consumer/energy/energy-supply/your-energy-meter/fixing-problems-with-your-smart-meters-in-home-display/).

You can find digital user guides for most types of IHD used in the UK. You can find them by manufacturer or by the number of buttons on the IHD.

## Find the user manual by the manufacturer of the IHD

This is the company that made the in-home display, not the supplier.

You might find a brand name or logo on the device that tells you who made it. You can find the user manual:

  * [if your IHD is made by Chameleon](/consumer/energy/energy-supply/your-energy-meter/find-the-user-guide-for-your-smart-meters-in-home-display/chameleon-in-home-displays/)

  * [if your IHD is made by GEO](/consumer/energy/energy-supply/your-energy-meter/find-the-user-guide-for-your-smart-meters-in-home-display/geo-in-home-displays/)

  * [if your IHD is made by In Home Displays](/consumer/energy/energy-supply/your-energy-meter/find-the-user-guide-for-your-smart-meters-in-home-display/in-home-displays/)

  * [if your IHD is made by Onzo](/consumer/energy/energy-supply/your-energy-meter/find-the-user-guide-for-your-smart-meters-in-home-display/onzo-in-home-displays/)

  * [if your IHD is made by Secure](/consumer/energy/energy-supply/your-energy-meter/find-the-user-guide-for-your-smart-meters-in-home-display/secure-in-home-displays/)

## Find the user manual by the number of buttons on the IHD

Choose the option that matches your in-home display. Don’t include power or reset buttons. You can find the user manual:

  * [if your IHD has no buttons (touchscreen only)](/consumer/energy/energy-supply/your-energy-meter/find-the-user-guide-for-your-smart-meters-in-home-display/if-your-ihd-has-no-buttons/)

  * [if your IHD has 1 to 3 buttons](/consumer/energy/energy-supply/your-energy-meter/find-the-user-guide-for-your-smart-meters-in-home-display/if-your-ihd-has-1-to-3-buttons/)

  * [if your IHD has 4 buttons](/consumer/energy/energy-supply/your-energy-meter/find-the-user-guide-for-your-smart-meters-in-home-display/if-your-ihd-has-4-buttons/)

  * [if your IHD has 5 buttons or more](/consumer/energy/energy-supply/your-energy-meter/find-the-user-guide-for-your-smart-meters-in-home-display/if-your-ihd-has-5-buttons-or-more/)
