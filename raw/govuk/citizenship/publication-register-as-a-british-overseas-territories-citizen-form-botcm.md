Form for use if you were born before 1 January 1983 and your mother would have been able to pass on British Overseas Territories Citizenship to you. The application will allow you to register as both a BOTC and a British citizen at the same time.

You should read:

  * the [BOTC(M) eligibility guidance](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/registration-as-british-if-your-mother-had-connection-to-a-british-overseas-territory)

  * the nationality forms guide [to help you fill in the form](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/nationality-forms-guide)

Send the form and [payment slip (including the fee)](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/fees-for-citizenship-applications) and your documents to:

UK Visas and Immigration at the following postal address:

Department 161  
UK Visas and Immigration  
The Capital Building  
New Hall Place  
Liverpool  
L3 9PP 

Paper forms only.
