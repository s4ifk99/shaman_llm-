In this collection you’ll find guidance for citizenship applications to:

  * register as a British national
  * naturalise as a British citizen
  * renounce/resume British citizenship/nationality

You can [check if you can become a British citizen](https://www.gov.uk/british-citizenship).

If you’re successful in your citizenship application, [find out what you can expect following your decision](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/british-citizenship-successful-applicants).
