You can apply to request [a citizenship affordability-based fee waiver online](http://www.gov.uk/get-child-citizenship-fee-waiver).

Use this form to request a citizenship affordability-based fee waiver if you are under the age of 18. You must submit a paper citizenship application alongside this form, or you can apply online.

You can find out who is eligible and how affordability will be assessed in the [citizenship fee waiver for individuals under 18 guidance](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/citizenship-fee-waiver-for-individuals-under-18-caseworker-guidance).
