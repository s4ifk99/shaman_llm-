If you do not live in the Channel Islands, Isle of Man or a British Overseas Territory, you [can apply online](https://visas-immigration.service.gov.uk/product/nationality-an).

You can [get help](https://www.gov.uk/assisted-digital-help-online-applications) to make your online application if you are in the UK and you do not have the appropriate access, skills or confidence to complete an online immigration application form.

Use this paper form if you live in the Channel Islands, Isle of Man or a British Overseas Territory, and want to apply for British citizenship by naturalisation.

You can also use this paper form if you live elsewhere and want to apply by post. You will need to submit your documents with your application.

Most people who live in the UK and apply online are able to keep their documents while their application is processed.

You should read:

  * the AN guidance [to check if you can apply](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/form-an-guidance)
  * the nationality forms guide [to help you fill in the form](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/nationality-forms-guide)

Send the form and [payment slip (including the fee)](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/fees-for-citizenship-applications) and your documents to:

  * the Lieutenant Governor if you are in the Channel Islands or Isle of Man
  * the Governor of the territory if you are in a British Overseas Territory
  * UK Visas and Immigration at the following postal address if you live elsewhere:

Department 1  
UK Visas and Immigration  
The Capital Building  
New Hall Place  
Liverpool  
L3 9PP 

## Application processing times

Our service level agreement is 6 months, but we make every effort to reach a decision on applications and provide an outcome as quickly as possible.

### Exceptions to standard processing times

There may be occasions when delays arise due to circumstances beyond our control and it is not possible to meet our agreed service level. If this happens we will keep you informed about the status of your application.
