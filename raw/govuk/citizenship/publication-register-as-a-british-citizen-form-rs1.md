You can [apply online](https://visas-immigration.service.gov.uk/product/nationality-rs1) unless you live in the Channel Islands, Isle of Man or a British Overseas Territory.

You can [get help with your online application](https://www.gov.uk/assisted-digital-help-online-applications) if you are in the UK and you do not have the appropriate access, skills or confidence to complete an online immigration application form.

Use this form if you live in the Channel Islands, Isle of Man or a British Overseas Territory and you previously renounced your British citizenship and now want to get it back.

You can also use this paper form if you live elsewhere and want to apply by post. You will need to submit your documents with your application.

Most people who live in the UK and apply online are able to keep their documents while their application is processed.

You should read:

  * the RS1 guidance [to check if you can apply](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/form-rs1-guidance)
  * the nationality forms guide [to help you fill in the form](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/nationality-forms-guide-channel-islands-isle-of-man-and-british-overseas-territories)

Send the form and [payment slip (including the fee)](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/fees-for-citizenship-applications) and your documents to:

  * the Lieutenant Governor if you are in the Channel Islands or Isle of Man
  * the Governor of the territory if you are in a British Overseas Territory
  * UK Visas and Immigration at the following postal address if you live elsewhere:

Department 1  
UK Visas and Immigration  
The Capital Building  
New Hall Place  
Liverpool  
L3 9PP
