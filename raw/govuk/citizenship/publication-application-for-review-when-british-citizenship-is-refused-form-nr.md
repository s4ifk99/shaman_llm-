Use this form if you have been refused citizenship or another type of nationality and you believe the decision was not soundly based on law, policy or procedure.

Send the completed form and [appropriate fee](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/fees-for-citizenship-applications) to:

Department 73  
UK Visas and Immigration  
The Capital  
New Hall Place  
Liverpool  
L3 9PP
