Guidance on registering for British citizenship using form B(OTA).

Use guide B1 if you’re a British Overseas Territories citizen.

Use guide B2 if you have lived in the UK for 5 years and you’re a:

  * British Overseas Territories citizen
  * British National (Overseas)
  * British Overseas citizen
  * British subject
  * British protected person

Use guide B3 if you’re an Irish citizen.
